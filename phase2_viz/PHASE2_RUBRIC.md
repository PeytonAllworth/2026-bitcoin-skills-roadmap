## üí° Self-Evaluation Tips

1. **Show charts to non-technical people** - Can they understand it?
2. **Compare to professional examples** - Check Bloomberg, FT, WSJ charts
3. **Print your charts** - Do they look good on paper?
4. **Wait 24 hours** - Does the chart still make sense?
5. **Check for honesty** - Are scales appropriate? Any misleading elements?
