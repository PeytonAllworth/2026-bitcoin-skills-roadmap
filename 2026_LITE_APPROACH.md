## üéì Learning Philosophy (2026)

### Use AI as a Teaching Assistant, Not a Crutch

**Good use of Cursor/AI:**
- "I wrote this regression code, can you check if my interpretation is correct?"
- "Here's my economic analysis, did I miss any key factors?"
- "I'm getting an error in this pandas code, what's wrong?"

**Bad use of Cursor/AI:**
- "Write a regression analysis for me" (you learn nothing)
- "Explain this entire topic to me" (just do the reading)
- "Build this model for me" (defeats the purpose)

### The 80/20 Rule

- You'll get 80% of the career value from 20% of the technical depth
- Master the INTERPRETATION and APPLICATION
- Leave the ENGINEERING and THEORY for later (if needed)

### Build Skills, Don't Collect Certifications

Your goal isn't to say "I can build production data pipelines."  
Your goal is to say "I can answer policy questions with data."
