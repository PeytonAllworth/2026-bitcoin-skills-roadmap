## Key Skills
- Structuring briefing memos (1-2 pages)
- Clear, concise policy writing
- Translating technical concepts for non-technical readers
- Understanding staffer needs

## Workflow
1. **Read examples:** Look at Cato, Coin Center, and Brookings memos
2. **Study the topic:** Understand The Clarity Act (or another policy issue)
3. **Outline first:** Before writing, create a clear structure
4. **Write rough draft:** Get your ideas down
5. **Edit ruthlessly:** Can you cut it in half and keep the key points?
6. **Test for clarity:** Would a busy staffer understand this in 3 minutes?

## What Makes a Good Policy Memo
- **Lead with the bottom line:** What's the key takeaway?
- **Use headers:** Make it scannable
- **Avoid jargon:** If you must use a technical term, define it
- **Be neutral:** Present facts and implications, not opinions
- **Be brief:** 1-2 pages for briefings, 3-5 for deep dives
- **Action-oriented:** What should the reader know or do?
